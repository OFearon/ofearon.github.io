Hello there! I'm Oonagh.

You may have already guessed, but I enjoy reading. I always have, but after I completed my undergrad in English Literature, I fell into a reading slump...and it lasted a long time.

Life took over and I forgot to read, and didn't actively make time for it. Fast forward a few years and I felt that starting to read would, quite simply, make me feel better. Liking a bit of structure in my life, I set myself a reading goal.

I started in 2017 with a modest goal of reading a book a month, to gently ease myself back into reading with a purpose and enjoying it. Having easily achieved that I set myself the more ambitious (for me) task of reading 50 books a year in 2018. It was a challenge that I enjoyed so much that I decided to aim for 50 again this year.

I enjoy reading a wide range of genres, my favourites are sci-fi, YA, in non-fiction I tend to gravitate towards buisness and self improvement books...I've also discovered a recent liking for books exploring health and heredity. My favourite author is Margaret Atwood (and no I've not watched The Handmaid's Tale yet!).
